The problem

RWA committees inherit the gate problem — and the liability.

When something goes wrong at the gate, the committee is accountable. But most gate systems give committees no visibility, no audit trail, and no control.

No audit trail when something goes wrong.

A theft, a trespass, an incident in the parking lot. The police ask for entry records. Your guard shows them a notebook with illegible handwriting from three days ago. The committee has no case.

"After a break-in, we had nothing to show the police. The guard's register had two pages of entries with no vehicle plates." — RWA Secretary, Bengaluru

Residents complain, committee has no data to respond.

'The guard let in delivery people without calling us.' 'Someone's car was in my spot.' 'My maid wasn't let in this morning.' Without event logs and photos, the committee is managing disputes with no evidence.

"Every AGM had the same complaints about the gate. We had no data to show what actually happened." — RWA Chairman, Pune

Dependent on a single guard's memory and judgment.

Your entire gate security model depends on one person being present, alert, and following procedure — every single day. That's not a system. That's a single point of failure.

"When our head guard went on leave, the substitute let in 3 unauthorized vehicles in 2 days. We found out a week later." — Facility Manager, Mumbai
How Dwaar AI solves it

One platform. Built for every challenge your gate faces.

Every entry logged — permanently, with photo.

Every vehicle, visitor and staff member entry is timestamped with a photo snapshot. Logs are stored permanently in the admin portal. Pull any date range in seconds for police, auditors, or AGM.

Committee dashboard — real-time gate visibility.

Committee members get a web dashboard showing live gate status, today's entries, any alerts, and gate health across all entry points. No need to call the guard to know what's happening.

Resident complaints resolved with evidence.

When a resident complains, pull the event log for that date and time. Photo, timestamp, method — all there. Disputes resolved in minutes, not meetings.

Gate runs without depending on any single guard.

Three-layer fallback means the gate keeps working regardless of guard shift changes, new staff, or temporary deployment. The system enforces the policy — not the guard's memory.

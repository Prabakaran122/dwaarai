Gate management for corporate offices & IT parks

Your employees deserve a gate that doesn't slow them down.

Dwaar AI gives your employees frictionless RFID entry, your visitors instant digital passes, and your security team a live compliance-ready log — all from one platform.

Employees clear in under 2 seconds — no stopping
Audit-ready logs — ISO, SOC, internal security

The problem

Your office gate is the first impression. Right now, it's a bottleneck.

Morning peak means 20 minutes lost before employees even get in.

Every car stops at the barrier. Guard checks ID or calls reception. Reception searches a spreadsheet. Gate opens. Repeat 200 times. Your office starts 20 minutes late every morning.

"Our parking gate queue was 40 cars by 9am. Employees were stressed before they even got to their desks." — Facilities Head, Bengaluru IT park

Visitor process is broken — paper books, no photo, no audit trail.

Visitors sign a paper register at reception. No photo, no ID check, no connection to the gate. Someone could write any name. Your "visitor log" proves nothing to an auditor.

"Our ISO auditor asked for visitor logs from 3 months ago. We had a notebook with illegible signatures." — Admin Manager, Mumbai corporate campus

Contractor access lives in spreadsheets and relies on a guard to enforce it.

You agreed a vendor can access Block B only on weekdays. That policy exists in an email. Your guard has a printed list. The list is 6 months old. And the vendor who resigned still has access.

"We found a contractor's vehicle in our secure lot 3 months after their contract ended. Nobody had updated the access list." — Security Manager, Delhi NCR campus
How Dwaar AI solves it

Frictionless entry. Controlled access. Compliance-ready logs.

RFID clears every employee vehicle before it stops.

Registered employee cars are identified from 3–5 metres. The barrier opens automatically. Zero stopping, zero guard check. Your 200-car morning peak takes 8 minutes, not 40.

Digital visitor passes — issued before the guest arrives.

Meeting hosts send visitors a digital pass from their phone or email. Visitor shows the OTP at the gate. Cleared in seconds, photo logged, audit trail created — no paper, no calls.

Contractor access — time-limited, instantly revocable.

Set start and end dates for contractor access. Restrict to specific gates or time windows. When the contract ends, revoke access from the admin portal. Effective immediately — no badge collection.

One-click audit reports — ISO, SOC, internal security.

Every gate event is logged with photo, timestamp, identification method and clearance status. Export as PDF or CSV for any date range. Your auditor gets clean data, not a notebook.

Common questions

What facility managers ask before every demo.

Dwaar AI installs RFID readers for registered employee vehicles, ANPR cameras for all vehicle logging, and facial detection for pedestrian staff gates. Employee vehicles clear automatically before stopping. Visitors receive digital OTP passes from their meeting host. Contractors are registered with time-limited, gate-specific access. All entries are logged with photo, timestamp and method — available for compliance reports.

For pre-booked visitors, the meeting host sends a digital OTP pass before the visitor arrives. When the visitor reaches the gate, the guard verifies the OTP on their tablet and sees the host name, location, and pass validity. For walk-in visitors, the guard registers them with a photo and vehicle plate, and issues a temporary pass. All visitor data is logged for audit — entry time, host, purpose, and vehicle plate.

Yes. Every gate event is logged with a timestamp, identification method, photo snapshot, and clearance status. These logs are stored in the admin portal and can be exported as PDF or CSV for any date range in one click. This satisfies visitor log requirements for ISO 27001, SOC 2, internal security audits, and incident investigation. Contractor visit history — including entry times, gates used, and dates — is also available as a report.

Contractors are registered in the admin portal with their vehicle plate and face photo. When the contract ends, access is revoked from the admin portal — effective immediately at the next gate scan. No badge collection needed. Full contractor visit history is retained for audit.

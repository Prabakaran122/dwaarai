The problem

Senior living gates face a unique challenge — security vs. accessibility.

Standard gate systems weren't designed for communities where residents are older, carers visit daily, and an ambulance delay is a life risk.

Ambulances and medical vehicles blocked by slow gates.

A paper register or slow RFID verification can cost critical minutes when every second matters. Standard gate systems treat ambulances the same as delivery vans — queue, check, clear. That's not acceptable in a senior living community.

"An ambulance waited 3 minutes at our gate because the guard couldn't reach the duty manager. We needed a better system." — Facility Director, Bangalore retirement community

Carers and nurses arriving multiple times a day — manual check every time.

Registered nurses, physiotherapists, and home carers visit daily — sometimes multiple times. Requiring phone calls or manual OTP every visit is friction that adds up. And each unverified entry is a security risk.

"Our nurse visits 12 residents a day across 3 buildings. The guard was calling our manager every time she went between buildings." — Operations Manager, Pune senior living

Family members can't get real-time notifications when someone visits their parent.

Adult children living away from their parents want to know when a visitor — or an ambulance — arrives. Current systems provide nothing. Families worry. And incidents get reported hours after they happen.

"My father had a fall. The ambulance came. I found out 2 hours later via a phone call." — Resident family member, Chennai
How Dwaar AI solves it

One platform. Built for every challenge your gate faces.

Emergency vehicles get priority — gate opens in seconds.

Registered ambulances, hospital vehicles and emergency services are identified by RFID or ANPR with a priority flag. The gate opens immediately on detection — no guard confirmation needed.

Carers and nurses face-verified — no daily OTP friction.

Registered care staff are face-identified at the gate on every visit. No phone calls, no OTP, no stopping. Entry logged with timestamp across every building they visit.

Family gets instant push notifications on every entry.

Family members receive real-time notifications when someone enters the community — visitor, carer, or emergency vehicle. Peace of mind, wherever they are.

Complete visitor and carer audit trail for compliance.

Every entry is logged with photo, timestamp, and method. Carer visit records, visitor logs, and emergency access reports are available for regulatory compliance.

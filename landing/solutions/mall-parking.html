The problem

Your parking experience is losing you customers.

Long entry queues, paper ticket confusion, and floor-full signs are the first experience your shoppers have. That sets the tone for the rest of their visit.

Token machine queues before shoppers even park.

5 cars waiting for tokens on a Sunday afternoon. Engine on, AC running, patience gone. By the time they park, their mood is already set. Tokenless ANPR isn't just faster — it redefines the first impression.

"We lose 8–10 minutes per shopper at peak just getting them through the entry barrier. That's time they're not spending in the mall." — Operations Head, Bengaluru mall

No real-time occupancy — shoppers circle floors looking for a spot.

Shoppers drive to floor 3, find it full, go to floor 4, find it full, and leave frustrated. Meanwhile your system shows parking as 'available' because it's counting capacity, not actual vehicles.

"We had 40% of our parking complaints come from shoppers who 'couldn't find a spot' — even when spots were available on other floors." — Mall Manager, Mumbai

Exit billing disputes and manual overrides waste staff time.

Ticket lost. Token unreadable. Timestamp dispute. Every manual override at exit is a queue, a dispute, and a revenue leak. And your shift supervisor has no record of what happened.

"Our parking staff spent 30% of their time handling billing exceptions. We had no logs, no photos, no audit trail." — Parking Manager, Delhi NCR
How Dwaar AI solves it

One platform. Built for every challenge your gate faces.

Tokenless ANPR — vehicle plate is the entry ticket.

ANPR cameras log every plate at entry. No token, no ticket, no stopping. The plate is the unique identifier. Exit fee is calculated by plate, not by a physical token that can be lost.

Real-time floor-wise occupancy — live dashboard and display.

Every entry and exit is counted per floor. Live occupancy data feeds the admin dashboard, digital signage at the entrance, and an API for your mall app. Shoppers see floor availability before they commit.

Season passes and VIP lanes — sub-2-second clearance.

Monthly season pass vehicles are registered by plate. VIP and corporate fleet vehicles get dedicated lanes. Cleared before they reach the barrier. Zero stopping, zero queue.

Exit audit log — every vehicle, every transaction.

Every vehicle's entry time, exit time, floor used, and fee status is logged with a photo snapshot. Exit billing disputes resolved in seconds with photographic evidence.

Common questions

Questions from commercial decision-makers.

Dwaar AI installs ANPR cameras at entry and exit lanes. When a vehicle arrives, the camera reads the number plate and logs the entry time. No token, no ticket, no stopping. At exit, the plate is read again, the system calculates the duration, generates the fee, and after payment the barrier opens. The plate is the ticket — nothing to lose, nothing to print.

Every entry and exit event increments or decrements the floor-wise vehicle count in real time. This data is available on the admin dashboard, can feed digital occupancy signage at each floor entrance, and can be accessed via API for integration with the mall's mobile app or website. Shoppers can see floor availability before they commit to a level.

Every vehicle entry and exit is logged with ANPR photo, timestamp, and camera ID. The parking manager can pull up the exact entry and exit photos with timestamps in seconds. Fee disputes are resolved with photographic evidence — no arguments, no manual overrides, no revenue leakage.
